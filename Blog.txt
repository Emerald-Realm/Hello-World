How to get ssh key
1. get gitbash
2.open gitbash
3. enter ssh-keygen -t rsa -b -4096 -C "githubemail@mail.com"
4. press enter
5. it ask's where to save, press enter to save at default
6. you are then asked ffor passphrase which serves as your password
7. you are needed to repeat the passphrase 
8. your key is generated
9.you go to the folder containing the key
10. you open the .pub file in a text editor
11. you can now copy and apply your key
